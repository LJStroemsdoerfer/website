Our Product

We developed the intelligent engine till that helps you to productionize your machine learning APIs. The till engine automatically builds your API into a container and smartly extracts all dependencies necessary to run your API. This allows you to fully focus on your machine learning development. However, till is totally transparent and you can at all times interact and edit the container.
