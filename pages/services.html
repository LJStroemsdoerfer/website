Data Science Community

Exchanging with peers a regular basis is one of the success factors in Data Science. The worst thing is to get stuck in a long-lasting project without touching Data Science topics outside of your project realm.

To foster and facilitate exchange among Data Scientists, especially around the topic of ML deployment, we setup a Data Science community Slack. Our users all get access to our channel. This way, we can react to feedback, give tips, but also learn from you. On top, you can exchange with your fellow Data Scientists on the latest trends and maybe challenges you are facing.

We use the community chat to schedule web sessions, discuss new features, offer support, receive feedback and send semi to really funny memes. If this sounds like something you would like, just approach us.
